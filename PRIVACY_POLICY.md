# Privacy Policy for Lichess Unicorn Knights

**Last updated: 2 March 2026**

Lichess Unicorn Knights does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The only data stored is your selected knight piece variant (unicorn, zebra, or dragon), saved locally in your browser using the Chrome `storage` API. This preference never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.
